About Us

You Lucky Dog is a third party organization that gives dog lovers access to meet local dogs that are currently displaced and looking for a lovely home. It is our mission to find amazing families for our furry friends as well as allow volunteers to foster a dog for a day. We have created a partnership with shelters near and far in order to display the available pets for adoption.
